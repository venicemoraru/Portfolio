A BUILDING TO CATCH!!
architecture, environmental learning centre
2024

In a state of ultimate rest, humans feed plants their breath. The carbon dioxide of a mindless exhale thus connects us to our ecosystems and benefits the environment — despite our passivity. Designed for RARE Conservation Area, A BUILDING TO CATCH!! explores this phenomenon as a pedagogical philosophy, aiming to turn innocent, unconscious moments of pause and activity into environmental education.
