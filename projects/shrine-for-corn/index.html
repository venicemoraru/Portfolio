Shrine for Corn
design-build
2022

The Shrine for Corn honours the crop as a symbol that connects Romanian and Anishinaabe culture. While inspired by the carpentry of Romanian shrines, the Shrine omits Christian imagery to evoke a shared, non-religious sense of divinity. It is built for disassembly and transportability, speaking to corn’s migrant journey from South America to Anishinaabe and Romanian land.
