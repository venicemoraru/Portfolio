Chair for a Singer
design-build, creative direction
2024

with Xanades Ivic-Williams

The hand-crafted chair is designed for a transient singer, shaped by her environment, grounded in her glamorous persona. As a frequent beachgoer, the singer can fold the chair and transport it to her favourite place of inspiration. Upon it she can lounge, contemplate, and form the words of her musical masterpieces while gently hovering above the sand.
