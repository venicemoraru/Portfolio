House in Kashii
architectural illustration
2022

with Yo Shimada / Tato Architects for GA Houses magazine

Drawn for Tato Architects’ article in the GA Houses Project 2020 magazine. The illustrations aim to explore indoor-outdoor connections of the firm’s House in Kashii project, while highlighting the home’s key architectural moments. The illustrations were featured on the cover of the issue.
