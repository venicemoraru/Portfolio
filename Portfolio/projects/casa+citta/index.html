Casa+Città
community design, urban design
2025

with Lauren Rueb

Casa+Cittá exists as a proposal for a vast site in the Portuense neighbourhood of Rome. The site houses a former soap factory, the Ex Mira Lanza, which is now officially abandoned — though informally settled and adapted by migratory inhabitants. Inspired by the innovative, unregulated world within the factory walls, Casa+Cittá tries not only to blur the lines between public and private space, but to support a lifestyle that thrives in this liminal realm. The project aims to amalgamate home and city, where visitors and inhabitants can access the amenities usually held within formally built housing, publicly.
